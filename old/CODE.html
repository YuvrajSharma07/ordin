Code@trix


Scrip@trix is a web-designing competition held at Ordin@trix.
The event is open for all classes.
Some Basic Rules Are-



No. of team(s) per school : 1


No. of participants per team : 1


Students will be required to create an animation on the given topic .


Topic will be announced on the day of the event.


Relevant images will be provided to the students.


Use of internet will not be allowed.


Time limit for the event will be 1.5 hours.
